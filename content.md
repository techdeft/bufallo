Buffalo Narrows Economic Development Corporation (BNEDC) was established in 1989 by the Northern Village of Buffalo Narrows to promote community business development and stimulate employment through various business ventures. The corporation has supported numerous projects, including the Town Center and a SARCAN depot, while also providing resources and services to local entrepreneurs.

Located on the picturesque shores of Churchill Lake in northwest Saskatchewan, Buffalo Narrows is a vibrant community with a population of approximately 1,100, primarily of Aboriginal descent. The area offers a wealth of recreational activities year-round, such as fishing, hunting, boating, and camping, making it an attractive destination for both residents and visitors.

About Us – Buffalo Narrows Economic Development Corporation (BNEDC)
The Buffalo Narrows Economic Development Corporation is committed to driving sustainable economic growth, community empowerment, and long-term prosperity for the people of Buffalo Narrows and the Northwest Saskatchewan region.

BNEDC works to create opportunities that strengthen the local economy, support entrepreneurship, attract investment, and improve the overall quality of life within the community. Through strategic partnerships, community engagement, and innovative development initiatives, the corporation continues to position Buffalo Narrows as a growing hub for business, tourism, culture, and regional collaboration.

Our mission is rooted in preserving our heritage, respecting our land, and building a stronger future for generations to come.
