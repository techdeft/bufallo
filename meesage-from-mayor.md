Message from the Mayor & Chairperson
Welcome to the Buffalo Narrows Economic Development Corporation and to the proud and resilient community of Buffalo Narrows.

As Mayor and Chairperson, I firmly believe that the future of Buffalo Narrows depends on bold leadership, strong partnerships, and a clear commitment to economic growth that benefits our people today and future generations tomorrow.

Our administration remains focused on building a stronger local economy, supporting infrastructure development, creating employment opportunities, attracting investment, and ensuring Buffalo Narrows has a strong voice in the future of Northern Saskatchewan development.

For too long, northern communities have often been overlooked despite contributing significantly to the economic strength of our province. Buffalo Narrows is changing that narrative. We are positioning ourselves as a strategic northern hub for tourism, mining support services, transportation, small business growth, Indigenous partnerships, and regional collaboration.

Through the work of BNEDC, we continue to advocate for investments that create real opportunities for our residents, strengthen local businesses, empower our youth, and improve the quality of life within our community. We understand that economic development is not just about projects and investments — it is about people, families, and building a future where our community can thrive independently and sustainably.

Our vision is clear:

To create jobs and opportunities locally

To support entrepreneurs and community businesses

To strengthen partnerships with Indigenous and regional stakeholders

To advocate for northern infrastructure and economic inclusion

To ensure Buffalo Narrows remains competitive, progressive, and investment-ready

We are committed to responsible leadership, transparency, and community-driven decision-making as we continue to move Buffalo Narrows forward.

The opportunities before us are significant, and with unity, determination, and strategic leadership, we will continue building a community that future generations will be proud to call home.

On behalf of Council and BNEDC, thank you for your continued support, belief, and partnership in the future of Buffalo Narrows.

Shaylee Gardiner
Mayor, Northern Village of Buffalo Narrows
Chairperson, Buffalo Narrows Economic Development Corporation
