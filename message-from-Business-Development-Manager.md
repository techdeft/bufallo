Message from the Business Development Manager
Welcome to the Buffalo Narrows Economic Development Corporation — where opportunity, community, and vision come together to shape a stronger future for Buffalo Narrows and Northwest Saskatchewan.

Buffalo Narrows is a community filled with resilience, potential, innovation, and pride. At BNEDC, we are proud to champion initiatives that create economic opportunities, strengthen local businesses, attract investment, empower our youth, and improve the overall wellbeing of our people.

We believe the future of Buffalo Narrows is bright.

Our community continues to grow as a destination for tourism, entrepreneurship, northern development, cultural experiences, and regional partnerships. With our strategic location, hardworking people, rich Indigenous heritage, and strong community spirit, Buffalo Narrows is positioned to become one of Northern Saskatchewan’s leading centres for sustainable economic growth and community development.

At BNEDC, our focus goes beyond business development — we are committed to building opportunities that inspire progress, create jobs, support families, encourage innovation, and leave a lasting impact for future generations.

Through partnerships with local organizations, Indigenous communities, government agencies, educational institutions, and private industry, we continue to advance projects and initiatives that strengthen our economy while preserving the values and identity that make Buffalo Narrows unique.

We are excited about the future and the possibilities ahead. Whether you are a resident, entrepreneur, investor, visitor, or potential partner, we welcome you to be part of the continued growth and success story of Buffalo Narrows.

Together, we are building a stronger community, a thriving economy, and a future filled with opportunity.

Thank you for visiting our website and learning more about our vision.

Oyewole Farinde
Business Development Manager
Buffalo Narrows Economic Development Corporation
